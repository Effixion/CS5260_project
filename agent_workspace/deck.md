## Slide 5: Key Takeaways & Recommended Actions (H1)

- H1 performance peaked in Mar–May (highest month: Apr ~$2.45M) and softened through Sep–Oct (~$1.95–$1.97M); plan demand-gen to counter the mid-year dip.
- Asia is the largest region (~$9.51M total), ahead of North America (~$8.68M) and Europe (~$7.96M); prioritize retention and expansion plays in Asia while targeting selective growth in NA.
- Electronics is the top category (~$9.41M) and the primary revenue lever; protect availability and launch cadence here to defend share.
- Home and Fashion are meaningful second-tier growth pools (~$6.19M and ~$5.59M); use targeted promos/bundles to lift conversion without over-discounting.
- Beauty is the smallest category (~$4.95M); refine assortment and shift spend toward highest-ROI SKUs rather than broad campaigns.
- Action: reallocate marketing and inventory toward Electronics + Asia in the next cycle, and run month-specific campaigns to smooth the late-summer/fall slowdown.
